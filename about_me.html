About Me

Professional Background

I am an education technologist with a background shaped by multiple education systems and a long-standing curiosity about how learning actually happens. My experiences in exam-driven classrooms and learner-centered environments led me to learning analytics as a way to understand, visualize, and support learning process beyond grades and test scores. I focus on using data to inform inclusive, human-centered instructional design and adaptive learning systems.

Career Aspirations & Goals

My career goal is tp bridge data, design, and pedagogy to creat more inclusive, personalized learning environments. I aim to work at the intersection of leanring analytics and instructional deisgn-leveraging data to understand learner behavior, support self-regulated learning, and inform evidence-based instructional decisions. Whether through EdTech platforms. research insititutions, or higher eductaion, I hope to design systems hat not only track learning, but empower it.
